Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zipytiny?

Zipytiny is an AI-powered learning platform that turns videos, PDFs, slide decks, articles, and notes into clear summaries, key concepts, and study-ready notes in seconds.

What file types can I upload to Zipytiny?

You can upload YouTube videos, local video files (MP4, WebM, MOV), audio recordings (MP3, WAV), PDF documents, slide presentations, articles, and written notes. Zipytiny extracts content and generates structured study material automatically.

How much does Zipytiny cost?

Zipytiny has a free plan to get started. The Pro plan is $12/month and the Enterprise plan is $39/month, with additional features at each tier.

Can Zipytiny turn content into PowerPoint presentations or flashcards?

Yes. Zipytiny generates active recall flashcards, interactive quizzes, visual mind maps, and allows Pro users to export video or document content directly into PowerPoint presentations.

Who is Zipytiny for?

Zipytiny is built for students, researchers, educators, and professionals who need to quickly turn long-form lectures, documents, and videos into concise, usable study notes.

Is there a free version of Zipytiny?

Yes, Zipytiny offers a free plan with core AI summarization and study note generation features.
