# Zipytiny Product Feature Backlog (Sprint 2+)

As part of Sprint 1, our absolute priority has been visual polish, stabilization, typography precision, performance, and search engine optimization (SEO) without introducing unsolicited feature bloat. 

To maintain strict scope discipline and preserve the premium, production-ready SaaS feel of Zipytiny, we have deferred the following feature opportunities to the official Sprint 2 backlog.

---

## 🗺️ Cognitive & Visual Enhancements
### 1. High-Resolution Vector Export for Mind Maps
* **Description:** Allow premium subscribers to export generated interactive mind maps as high-resolution SVGs or print-ready PDFs.
* **Impact:** High utility for students preparing physical study walls or including diagrams in course assignments.
* **Tech Stack:** SVG serializer integrated with `d3`-based rendering.

### 2. Live Spatial Map Clustering
* **Description:** Automatically group related video nodes dynamically when dragging items on the visual mind map canvas.
* **Impact:** Enhances spatial learning mechanics.

---

## 📇 Active Recall & Flashcard Decks
### 3. Native Anki & Quizlet Direct Export
* **Description:** Direct export of spaced-repetition flashcards generated from PDFs and lectures to native `.apkg` (Anki) and CSV format for Quizlet.
* **Impact:** Direct conversion booster for heavy academic users who rely on dedicated spaced-repetition software.

### 4. Spaced Repetition Scheduler (SuperMemo-2 Algorithm)
* **Description:** An in-app smart algorithm that prompts users to review specific card decks exactly 1, 3, 7, and 14 days after creation.
* **Impact:** Dramatically improves user retention and daily active users (DAU).

---

## 🎙️ Rich Media & Audio AI
### 5. Multi-Voice Studio Summaries (Podcast Mode)
* **Description:** Convert text summaries into realistic premium dual-host podcasts explaining the lecture content.
* **Impact:** Ideal for auditory learners who consume content during transit or workout sessions.

### 6. Interactive Video Annotation Overlay
* **Description:** Overlay dynamic AI chapter notes directly on top of the floating video preview, synchronized to the current timestamp.
* **Impact:** Keeps the user focused entirely within one learning timeline.

---

## 🤝 Collaborative & Institutional Workspace
### 7. Shared Team Study Guides
* **Description:** Enable shared real-time workspaces where multiple students or team members can annotate summaries, compile group flashcards, and live-chat.
* **Impact:** Unlocks high-conversion B2B university and corporate accounts.

---

## 🔌 Platform Integrations
### 8. Browser Extension Smart-Capture Widget
* **Description:** A browser companion extension that lets users instantly send any webpage, YouTube video, or lecture PDF straight to their Zipytiny workspace with one click.
* **Impact:** Drastically reduces user friction, driving seamless organic user acquisition.
